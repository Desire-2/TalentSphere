# AfriTech Opportunities - SEO Keyword Research & Strategy

## Overview

This document provides comprehensive keyword research for **AfriTech Opportunities**, a job and scholarship portal targeting Rwanda and Africa-wide audiences.

---

## Target Markets & Audiences

### Primary Markets
1. **Rwanda** - Local job seekers, students, employers
2. **East Africa** - Regional professionals and scholars
3. **Pan-Africa** - Diaspora and African professionals globally

### Target Audiences
1. **Job Seekers** - Active job search, career changers
2. **Students** - Scholarship hunters, intern seekers
3. **Professionals** - Mid-career, executives, remote workers
4. **Employers** - Small, medium, large companies
5. **Universities** - Scholarship program partners
